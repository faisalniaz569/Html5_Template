About us

About us:

The Willow Restaurant founded in 2010 has become one of the leading names in Washington Court House, OH for serving the finest American cuisine. We are known for our home cooking, just like Grandma makes. We have a great reputation in the area, especially for our ribs, steaks and homemade pies. If you are in the city, then don’t miss out on our grand pizza buffet which gives you a wide choice of pizzas at a great price. In addition to our restaurant services, we also provide catering to all kinds of events from birthdays to weddings.

Willow Restaurant and Bar (Sacramento, CA): Situated within The Exchange, a Curio Collection by Hilton hotel, Willow offers a casual fine dining experience inspired by Southern Italian and Mediterranean cuisines. The focus is on house-made pastas, seafood, and game proteins. The bar menu boasts handcrafted cocktails and a curated wine collection, making it a perfect spot for both travelers and locals.
