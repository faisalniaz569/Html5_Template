Willow:

Willow Restaurant & Bar invites you to indulge in a refined dining experience, through Southern Italian and Mediterranean Sea-inspired cuisine. Under the expert guidance of Executive Chef Scott Carr, Willow masterfully blends the decadence of house-made breads and pastas with the vibrant flavors of Mediterranean seafood and spices.

History:

Designed by the renowned architect Charles Rennie Mackintosh, the Willow Tearooms opened in 1903 at 217 Sauchiehall Street. Commissioned by entrepreneur Catherine Cranston, these tearooms became iconic examples of Art Nouveau design. Over the years, the building underwent various changes but was meticulously restored between 2014 and 2018. In January 2024, the National Trust for Scotland acquired the tearooms, ensuring their preservation as a significant piece of Glasgow's cultural heritage.
